About

Where apparel design meets
mechanical engineering.

I specialize in the development and manufacturing of high-performance technical softgoods, with an extensive background in translating complex design intent into reliable, manufacturable solutions. My work bridges the critical intersection of apparel design, mechanical engineering, and product development — ensuring that aesthetics and function meet technical specifications.

My expertise is rooted in the full product lifecycle: from initial conceptualization and 3D surface development to 2D pattern engineering, material utilization, and production support. I thrive in environments that demand precision, leveraging advanced CAD platforms including Siemens NX, CATIA 3DEXPERIENCE, and Rhino 3D to resolve fit, geometry, and process challenges before tooling begins.

Throughout my career I have successfully applied engineering discipline to tactical gear, medical wearables, and aerospace components. Whether managing complex grading challenges or optimizing manufacturing workflows, my focus remains on risk reduction, repeatability, and the modernization of technical development processes.
